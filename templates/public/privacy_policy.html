Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

Overview

This policy explains how the Whistleblowing Report System collects, uses, and protects information submitted through the platform. By submitting a report or signing in as an investigator you agree to the practices described below.

What We Collect

  • Report details, supporting files, and optional contact information provided by reporters.
  • Account information for administrators and investigators (name, email, role).
  • Audit metadata such as timestamps, assignment history, and status changes.

How We Use Your Information

  • Route and investigate reports responsibly.
  • Maintain an auditable history of case activity.
  • Notify authorized users about updates that require their attention.

Data Security

Access to the dashboard is restricted to authenticated investigators and administrators. All passwords are stored as secure hashes and file uploads are kept inside the protected application directory. Regular reviews of access logs and assignments are required to safeguard sensitive information.

Your Choices

  • Reporters may submit anonymously; providing contact details is optional.
  • Investigators may request that reports be reassigned or removed according to internal policy.
  • Administrators can delete user accounts and uploaded attachments when retention requirements expire.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to your compliance or legal team. For technical issues please use the contact methods supplied by your organization.
